Gibb Painting

Providing top quality painting and restoration services to the greater Richmond area

Welcome to Gibb Painting, Richmond, Virginia's premier painting and restoration company. Since 2002, owner Timothy Gibb has built the company from the ground up with a primary focus on quality. Gibb Painting takes pride in restoring Richmond homes, historic and otherwise, to their original beauty.

Services About Gallery Contact

Exterior Painting

Exterior painting often includes powerwashing and paint removal in the form of scraping, sanding, or ultraviolet paint removal.

Interior Painting

Interior painting includes color consultation and may require wallpaper removal, plaster repair, and/or drywall repair.

Carpentry

Carpentry work ranges from custom-built specialty items such as wine racks, to simple repair or siding or remediation of rot.

Furniture and Cabinetry

There are several options for furniture and cabinetry painting / finishing, including spray, specialty finishes, and hand-painting.

Decorative Painting & Faux Finishing

Decorative painting and faux finishing may be applied to walls, cabinetry, furniture, you name it. Finish types include marble, distressed, antiqued, and more.

About

Gibb Painting is available to bid on residential and commercial painting and restoration work in Richmond. A graduate of VCU's College of Art, Tim Gibb has an extensive knowledge of architecture, color and aesthetics. He offers educated input on color choices, products, trim styles, etc. to give your project a distinctive look. With a long history of satisfied and returning clients, Gibb Painting is happy to supply references displaying a variety of work. A key part of the business is to remain a small, manageable size in order to properly focus on one job at a time, unlike big ticket contractors who look to expedite jobs for quick and easy profits. Gibb is a hands-on owner working beside his employees daily to ensure that the best work is done for maximum longevity and beauty for your home.
